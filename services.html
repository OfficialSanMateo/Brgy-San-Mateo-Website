Our Services

Services we provide for the barangay of
San Mateo San Pablo City, Laguna

Barangay Officials

Primary Planning

We prioritize community needs through Primary Planning We engage residents, gather insights, and identify development areas. Collaboratively, we plan projects and services to meet people's priorities, laying the groundwork for inclusive and sustainable development that enhances the well-being of all constituents.

Implementing Policies

We enforce policies set by the local government to ensure community safety and well-being. We manage programs, oversee projects, and ensure compliance with guidelines for a thriving barangay.

Program

We develop and implement programs to address various needs within our community. These initiatives cover areas such as health, education, livelihood, and infrastructure. Through our programs, we aim to improve the lives of our residents and promote the overall development and well-being of our barangay.

Project

We initiate and manage programs that meet diverse needs in our community. These projects, which range from infrastructural upgrades to social services, are intended to improve the quality of life for our people while also promoting general barangay development.
